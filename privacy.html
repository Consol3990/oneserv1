Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026  ·  Consolidated Assets LLC d/b/a OneServ  ·  Arizona

The short version: We collect only what we need to run your account. We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising. We never have and never will. We built this for tradespeople — not to monetize your information.

Who We Are

OneServ is a field service management application operated by Consolidated Assets LLC, an Arizona limited liability company. References to "OneServ," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Consolidated Assets LLC.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at [email protected].

What Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide when you create an account or use the application:

  • Account information: First name, last name, business name, trade type, email address, phone number, and number of technicians
  • Payment information: Billing is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number. Stripe handles all payment data under their own privacy policy.
  • Job and dispatch data: Information you enter into the app about your jobs, customers, and technicians. This is your business data — it belongs to you.
  • Usage data: Basic technical information such as browser type and login timestamps, used only for security and to keep the app running properly.

We do not collect data about your customers beyond what you choose to enter. We do not run analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling on your account activity.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone, ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing platforms.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • We do not send you marketing emails from third parties.
  • We do not build profiles on you for any purpose other than running your account.

Third-Party Services We Use

To operate OneServ we use the following infrastructure providers. Each handles data under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Twilio — SMS notifications sent to your technicians
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (account confirmations, invoices)
  • Vercel — application hosting

These providers are used solely to deliver the OneServ service. We do not share your data with them beyond what is technically necessary to operate the application.

How We Protect Your Data

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access to your account data is restricted to you and, where technically necessary, to Consolidated Assets LLC for support and maintenance purposes.

Your Data Is Yours

You own your business data. You can request a full export of your account data at any time by emailing [email protected]. If you cancel your account, we will delete your data within 30 days upon written request.

Cookies

OneServ uses only functional cookies necessary to keep you logged in and the application running. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Children's Privacy

OneServ is a business application intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will notify active users by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most current version.
