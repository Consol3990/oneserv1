The short version: We collect only what we need to run your account. We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising. We never have and never will. We built this for tradespeople — not to monetize your information.

Who We Are

OneServ is a field service management application operated by Consolidated Assets LLC, an Arizona limited liability company. References to "OneServ," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Consolidated Assets LLC.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at dutch@oneserv.app.

What Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide when you create an account or use the application:

  • Account information: First name, last name, business name, trade type, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment information: Billing is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number.
  • Job and dispatch data: Information you enter about your jobs, customers, and technicians. This is your business data — it belongs to you.
  • Usage data: Basic technical information such as browser type and login timestamps, used only for security and to keep the app running properly.

We do not collect data about your customers beyond what you choose to enter. We do not run analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling on your account activity.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone, ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing platforms.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • We do not send you marketing emails from third parties.
  • We do not build profiles on you for any purpose other than running your account.

SMS / Text Message Communications

OneServ SMS Program — Full Disclosure

Program description: OneServ operates an SMS messaging program for two distinct purposes:

  • Platform communications: Informational SMS messages sent directly to OneServ subscribers (business owners and their staff) regarding their OneServ account, trial status, onboarding, and platform updates.
  • Customer-care notifications on behalf of subscribers: OneServ enables home service businesses (plumbers, HVAC, electricians, and other trades) to send informational/customer-care SMS notifications to their own end customers. These messages include appointment confirmations, technician en route alerts, job completion notices, invoice delivery, and review requests. These messages are sent on behalf of the service business, not by OneServ for marketing purposes.

How consent is obtained:

  • OneServ subscribers consent to receive SMS communications from OneServ at the time of account creation by checking an explicit opt-in checkbox on the signup form at go.oneserv.app/signup.
  • End customers of home service businesses consent to receive customer-care SMS notifications from their service provider at the point of service — either via an in-app opt-in checkbox when their contact information is entered, or by providing their phone number directly to the service business with acknowledgment of SMS communications.
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase. Opting out does not affect access to services.

Types of messages sent: All SMS messages are informational/customer-care in nature. Message types include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Technician en route alerts (e.g. "Your technician is on the way")
  • Job completion notices
  • Invoice delivery and payment notifications
  • Review requests following completed service
  • Account and trial status updates (to OneServ subscribers only)

OneServ does not send promotional or marketing SMS messages. All messages are transactional or customer-care in nature.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies. OneServ subscribers may receive up to 5 messages per month related to their account. End customers of service businesses may receive messages related to their active service appointments — typically 2–5 messages per service visit.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier and plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. You may also opt out by emailing dutch@oneserv.app or calling 520-444-7488.

To get help: Reply HELP to any text message, or contact us at dutch@oneserv.app or 520-444-7488.

No sharing of opt-in data: Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Opt-in data is used solely to deliver the described customer-care and informational messages. Data may be shared with Twilio (our SMS carrier/aggregator) solely for message delivery purposes.

Carriers: Supported carriers include AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
