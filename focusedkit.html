LinkedIn Post Formula — Every Post Follows This Structure

Line 1 — The Hook
One sentence. Bold claim, surprising fact, or relatable question. This is the only thing people see before they click "see more." Make them click.

Lines 2–4 — Context
Set up the problem or situation. Make it relatable. Keep sentences short — 1 line each. White space is your friend on LinkedIn.

Body — The Value
Numbered list, bullet points, or short paragraphs. Max 2–3 lines per paragraph. Never write a wall of text.

Closing — Soft CTA
Invite engagement OR offer a next step. Never hard sell. "What's worked for you?" or "Try it free: go.oneserv.app" — not both.
